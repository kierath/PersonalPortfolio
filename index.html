About Me

Hello, my name is Kierath. This section will be an overview of my background.

Growing up I had always been interested in gaming and technology, with this passion I decided that I would like to study in this area. In 2020 I graduated from a BSc(Hons) in Games Design and Development and 2021 in MA Computer Games: Art and Design. I then went on to do an internship as a 3D Generalist where I practiced my 3D art skills.

Whilst doing my internship, I found there wasn't much progression. Looking back to my university degrees I enjoyed coding and the challenges that came with it. Hence, I decided to complete this coding course and begin a career within coding.

My dream has always been to work within the gaming industry. I believe once I have the right set of skills I hope to become a software engineer for a gaming company.
